# 📊 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end HR analytics project built with Power BI, uncovering why employees
leave and helping stakeholders make data-driven retention decisions.

## 🚀 Project Overview
Acting as a Data Analyst, I investigated employee attrition across departments,
job roles, salary bands, and age groups — going beyond charts to deliver real
business insights.

**Key findings:**
- Overall attrition rate: **16.1%** across 1,470 employees
- Highest attrition in the **26–35 age group** (116 employees)
- **163 out of 237** employees who left earned under $5K/month
- **Laboratory Technicians** had the highest attrition by job role (62)
- Attrition drops sharply as salary hike % increases
